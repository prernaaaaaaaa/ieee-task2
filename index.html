IEEE IES Generative AI Hackathon

IEEE IES Generative AI Hackathon has set its sights on solving real-world problems using Generative AI solutions.It is an exciting event that brings together creative minds, developers, designers, and innovators to collaborate and build amazing projects. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, this hackathon is the perfect opportunity to showcase your skills, learn new technologies, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions

1) What is the IEEE IES Generative AI Hackathon?

The IEEE IES Generative AI Hackathon is a competition organized by the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society. It focuses on innovative applications of Generative AI in industrial electronics and related fields

2) Who can participate in the hackathon?

The hackathon is open to students, professionals, and researchers from around the world. Participants can be individuals or part of a team.

3) How do I register for the hackathon?

You can register through the official hackathon website. Ensure you provide accurate details, including your contact information and team members' names if applicable.

4) Are there any prerequisites for participating?

Participants should have a basic understanding of Generative AI concepts and programming skills. Familiarity with industrial electronics is beneficial but not required.

5) How will the projects be judged?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, technical complexity, feasibility, and impact. A panel of experts from academia and industry will evaluate the submissions.

6) What are the prizes for the winners?

Prizes may include cash awards, certificates, opportunities for publication, and potential collaboration or internship offers from sponsoring companies.
